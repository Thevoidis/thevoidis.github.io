Dear Kafka

What is this this story about ? Justice ? Depression ? Delusion ? Hallucination ? I can’t tell.

Chapter 1
Kyton The eyes were always there. Not real eyes, not reflections in a rain-slicked window or the passing glance of a stranger on the street. These were heavier. They pressed against the inside of his skull, a phantom weight behind his own gaze. Freedom? Freedom was a word for people who didn’t carry their own courtroom inside their head. Kyton was a man who could disappear any day. No one would notice or care.
